Frequently Asked

Questions & Answers

Do I need a special license to drive the Sprinter?

No special license required! A standard driver's license is sufficient. The van is easy to drive and handles like a large SUV. Just watch out for low hanging branches :-P!

What's included with the van rental?

Everything you need for adventure: queen memory foam bed, linens, cookware, induction cooktop, fridge, solar power, heater, toilet, and camping chairs/table. Just bring your clothes and food!

Can I rent just the suite, or just the van?

Yes! You can rent either separately or combine them for the ultimate experience. Guests use the suite as a staging area before hitting the road.

Is the van suitable for winter camping?

Absolutely! The van is 4-season ready with a space heater, MaxxFan, Velit AC, wool insulation, and oversized all-terrain tires (chains included). Perfect for Tahoe winters and desert summers.

How close are you to Lake Tahoe?

We're located in Reno, just 35 miles (45 minutes) from Lake Tahoe. Perfect for day trips to the lake or extended mountain adventures.
